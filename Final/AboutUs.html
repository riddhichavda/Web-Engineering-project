We are Specialist Wedding & Celebration Cake Makers, Designers and Suppliers based in Arcade Market, Churchgate. We also have another branch in Lower Parel. We supply high quality Regular, Wedding, Occasion, Kids’ Special, Fruit and Cheese cakes, & Special cakes. We also attract many customers that travel from across Mumbai to get one of our High Quality Celebration Cakes. In fact we have the ability to make a bespoke celebration cake for any occasion. Almost all of our Novelty / Character cakes are hand-cut, we also hand mould our own sugar-paste models, as well as making a selection of handmade Sugar Flowers, we can offer a complete bespoke service. These are all baked in tins for a more perfect shape. Our cakes are suitable for vegetarians and we also bake to our own recipe Gluten/Wheat & Dairy Free Cakes. All our Cakes are baked with traditional ingredients in our own on-site bakery. You will get that homemade taste & flavor from our cakes. Our Cakes are sold exclusively through our OWN shop by experienced sales staff and are not available other high street stores or supermarkets. You can design your own wedding or other celebration cake with us or choose a previously designed cake as seen on this site. Our pages are full of party ideas and cake designs. There is no need to book an appointment the shop is open 6 days a week. For more information on how to find us, check our Contact Us page.
